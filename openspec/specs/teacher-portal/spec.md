## Purpose

The teacher portal provides educators with comprehensive tools to manage courses, create and grade assessments, monitor student progress, receive notifications, and communicate with students and parents.
## Requirements
### Requirement: Teacher Dashboard

Teachers SHALL have a management dashboard.

#### Scenario: View dashboard

- **WHEN** a teacher logs in
- **THEN** their courses and students are overviewed

### Requirement: Course Management

Teachers SHALL manage their courses.

#### Scenario: Edit course

- **WHEN** a teacher selects a course
- **THEN** editing tools are available

### Requirement: Student Oversight

Teachers SHALL monitor student progress.

#### Scenario: View student progress

- **WHEN** in the portal
- **THEN** student data and analytics are shown

### Requirement: Assessment Management

Teachers SHALL create and manage assessments for their courses.

#### Scenario: Create assessment

- **WHEN** a teacher selects a course
- **THEN** assessment creation tools are available

### Requirement: Notifications

Teachers SHALL receive notifications about student activity and system updates.

#### Scenario: View notifications

- **WHEN** in the portal
- **THEN** notifications are displayed

### Requirement: Communication Tools

Teachers SHALL communicate with students and parents.

#### Scenario: Send message

- **WHEN** a teacher sends a message
- **THEN** the recipient receives it
