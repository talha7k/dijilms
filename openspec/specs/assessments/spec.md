## Requirements
### Requirement: Quiz Creation

Instructors SHALL be able to create quizzes with questions.

#### Scenario: Instructor creates quiz

- **WHEN** an instructor adds questions to a quiz
- **THEN** the quiz is saved and can be assigned to lessons

### Requirement: Assessment Taking

Students SHALL be able to take assessments.

#### Scenario: Student takes quiz

- **WHEN** a student starts a quiz
- **THEN** questions are presented and answers recorded

### Requirement: Automatic Scoring

The system SHALL automatically score quizzes.

#### Scenario: Quiz submission scored

- **WHEN** a student submits a quiz
- **THEN** the score is calculated and stored
