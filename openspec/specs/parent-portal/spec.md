## Purpose

The parent portal enables guardians to view their child's learning progress, assessment results, receive notifications, and communicate with teachers.
## Requirements
### Requirement: Parent Access

Parents SHALL access their child's learning data.

#### Scenario: Parent logs in

- **WHEN** a parent authenticates
- **THEN** they see their child's dashboard

### Requirement: Progress Viewing

Parents SHALL view their child's progress.

#### Scenario: Parent checks progress

- **WHEN** a parent views the portal
- **THEN** child's course progress is shown

### Requirement: Communication

Parents SHALL communicate with teachers.

#### Scenario: Parent messages teacher

- **WHEN** a parent sends a message
- **THEN** the teacher receives it

### Requirement: Assessment Viewing

Parents SHALL view their child's assessment results.

#### Scenario: View assessments

- **WHEN** a parent checks their child's progress
- **THEN** assessment scores are shown

### Requirement: Notifications

Parents SHALL receive notifications about their child's progress.

#### Scenario: View notifications

- **WHEN** in the portal
- **THEN** notifications are displayed
