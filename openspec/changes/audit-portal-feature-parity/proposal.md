## Why

To ensure all portals have access to relevant features for a consistent and complete user experience across teacher, student, and parent roles.

## What Changes

- Add assessments functionality: management for teachers, taking for students, viewing for parents.
- Add notifications to all portals (teacher, student, parent).
- Add communication tools to teacher and student portals.
- Verify and enhance progress tracking consistency across portals.

## Impact

- Affected specs: teacher-portal, student-portal, parent-portal
- Affected code: Portal components, routes, and integrations with assessments, notifications, and communication systems.
