## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Assessment Management

Teachers SHALL create and manage assessments for their courses.

#### Scenario: Create assessment

- **WHEN** a teacher selects a course
- **THEN** assessment creation tools are available

### Requirement: Notifications

Teachers SHALL receive notifications about student activity and system updates.

#### Scenario: View notifications

- **WHEN** in the portal
- **THEN** notifications are displayed

### Requirement: Communication Tools

Teachers SHALL communicate with students and parents.

#### Scenario: Send message

- **WHEN** a teacher sends a message
- **THEN** the recipient receives it
