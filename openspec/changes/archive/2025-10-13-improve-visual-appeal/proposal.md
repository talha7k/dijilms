## Why

The website lacks visual appeal with poor contrast on buttons, no navbar active indicators, and minimal use of brand colors on key pages.

## What Changes

- Improve button contrast by adjusting theme colors.
- Add active state indicators to marketing navbar.
- Incorporate green and gold brand colors more prominently on home, features, and pricing pages.

## Impact

- Affected code: src/lib/components/marketing/nav/main-nav.svelte, src/app.css, marketing page components
- Enhances user experience and brand consistency.
