## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Student Dashboard

Students SHALL have a personalized dashboard.

#### Scenario: View dashboard

- **WHEN** a student logs in
- **THEN** their courses and progress are displayed

### Requirement: Course Access

Students SHALL access enrolled courses.

#### Scenario: Open course

- **WHEN** a student selects a course
- **THEN** lessons and content are available

### Requirement: Progress Viewing

Students SHALL view their learning progress.

#### Scenario: Check progress

- **WHEN** in the portal
- **THEN** progress bars and completion status are shown
