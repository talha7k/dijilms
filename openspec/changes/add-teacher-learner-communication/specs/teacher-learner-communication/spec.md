## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Direct Messaging

Teachers and learners SHALL exchange direct messages.

#### Scenario: Teacher sends message

- **WHEN** a teacher sends a message to a student
- **THEN** the student receives it

### Requirement: Announcements

Teachers SHALL send announcements to classes.

#### Scenario: Class announcement

- **WHEN** a teacher posts an announcement
- **THEN** all enrolled students see it

### Requirement: Group Communication

The system SHALL support group discussions.

#### Scenario: Group chat

- **WHEN** users join a group
- **THEN** they can communicate collectively
