## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Lesson Progress

The system SHALL track completion of individual lessons.

#### Scenario: Student completes lesson

- **WHEN** a student finishes viewing content
- **THEN** the lesson is marked as completed

### Requirement: Progress Display

Users SHALL see their progress in courses and modules.

#### Scenario: User views progress

- **WHEN** a user checks their course dashboard
- **THEN** progress bars and percentages are shown

### Requirement: Course Completion

The system SHALL track overall course completion.

#### Scenario: Course completed

- **WHEN** all lessons in a course are completed
- **THEN** the course is marked as completed
