## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Behavior Tracking

The system SHALL track student behaviors.

#### Scenario: Log behavior

- **WHEN** a teacher records a behavior
- **THEN** it is stored in the log

### Requirement: Rewards and Punishments

Teachers SHALL issue rewards or punishments.

#### Scenario: Issue reward

- **WHEN** a teacher gives a reward
- **THEN** the student's record is updated

### Requirement: Behavior Reports

Users SHALL view behavior logs.

#### Scenario: View behavior history

- **WHEN** accessing behavior reports
- **THEN** logs are displayed
