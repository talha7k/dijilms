## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Certificate Generation

The system SHALL generate certificates for completed courses.

#### Scenario: Course completion certificate

- **WHEN** a student completes a course
- **THEN** a certificate is generated

### Requirement: Certificate Access

Users SHALL be able to view and download their certificates.

#### Scenario: User views certificate

- **WHEN** a user accesses their certificates
- **THEN** they can view and download them

### Requirement: Certificate Verification

Certificates SHALL be verifiable.

#### Scenario: Verify certificate

- **WHEN** someone checks a certificate ID
- **THEN** its validity is confirmed
