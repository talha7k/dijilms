## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Management Dashboard

Management SHALL have an oversight dashboard.

#### Scenario: View dashboard

- **WHEN** management logs in
- **THEN** system metrics and approvals are displayed

### Requirement: Course Approvals

Management SHALL approve or reject courses.

#### Scenario: Review course

- **WHEN** a course is submitted
- **THEN** approval tools are available

### Requirement: System Analytics

Management SHALL view institution-wide data.

#### Scenario: Access analytics

- **WHEN** in the portal
- **THEN** comprehensive reports are shown
