## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Course Review

Management SHALL review teacher-created courses.

#### Scenario: Submit for review

- **WHEN** a teacher submits a course
- **THEN** it enters review queue

### Requirement: Feedback Provision

Reviewers SHALL provide feedback.

#### Scenario: Give feedback

- **WHEN** a reviewer evaluates
- **THEN** feedback is sent to teacher

### Requirement: Approval Process

Courses SHALL be approved or rejected.

#### Scenario: Approve course

- **WHEN** a reviewer approves
- **THEN** the course is published
