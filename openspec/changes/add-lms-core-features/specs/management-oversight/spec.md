## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Content Review
Management (HOD, Principal) SHALL be able to review all teacher-created courses and content.

#### Scenario: Accessing content for review
- **WHEN** management user navigates to content review dashboard
- **THEN** list of pending/unreviewed courses is displayed

### Requirement: Feedback Provision
Management SHALL provide feedback on content quality and suggest improvements.

#### Scenario: Submitting feedback
- **WHEN** management reviews content and submits feedback
- **THEN** feedback is sent to the teacher and status updated

### Requirement: Content Approval
Management SHALL approve or reject content changes.

#### Scenario: Approving content
- **WHEN** management approves reviewed content
- **THEN** content becomes publicly available

### Requirement: Change Tracking
All content modifications SHALL be tracked with management oversight.

#### Scenario: Viewing change history
- **WHEN** management views content history
- **THEN** all changes, feedback, and approvals are shown
