## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Content Upload

Instructors SHALL be able to upload various content types to courses.

#### Scenario: Instructor uploads video

- **WHEN** an instructor uploads a video file
- **THEN** it is stored securely and linked to the lesson

### Requirement: Content Viewing

Students SHALL be able to view content securely.

#### Scenario: Student accesses content

- **WHEN** a student opens a lesson with content
- **THEN** the content is displayed with appropriate viewer

### Requirement: Secure Access

Content SHALL only be accessible to enrolled students.

#### Scenario: Unauthorized access blocked

- **WHEN** a non-enrolled user tries to access content
- **THEN** access is denied
